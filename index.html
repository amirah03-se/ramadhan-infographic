The Three Phases of Ramadan:
First 10 Days — Mercy
Second 10 Days — Forgiveness
Last 10 Days — Freedom

🌙 Phases of Ramadan
🌿 First 10 Days — Mercy The doors of Allah's mercy are widely opened. A time to begin positive change and seek closeness to Him.
🤍 Second 10 Days — Forgiveness A phase where Allah forgives the sins of those who sincerely repent and strive to improve themselves.
✨ Last 10 Days — Freedom from the Hellfire The most special period to increase worship and seek Laylatul Qadr, hoping for protection from punishment.
